Eagle Brand Chocolate Fondue

Description

Chocolate fondue is so easy to make. Don't just save this elegant dessert idea for special

gatherings â€” enjoy it every day with your family. Kids will enjoy dipping fruits and cookies

into this chocolate treat for an after-school snack. Serve warm as a dipping sauce, or

drizzle it warm or cold over ice cream or cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated milk)
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Combine condensed milk, chocolate chips, butter, and water in a heavy saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir until chocolate chips and butter melt, then continue to cook, stirring constantly until thickened, about 5 more minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in vanilla.
  4. Serve
  5. Enjoy!
